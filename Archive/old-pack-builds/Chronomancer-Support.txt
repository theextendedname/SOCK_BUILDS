- More info
 - Primary goal: Provide boons and healing to party.
 - Secondary goal: Revive downs with Illusion of Life.

 - Boons
  - Stability --> Distortion (F4) and Mantra of Concentration
  - Alacrity --> on any shatter
  - Quickness --> Time Warp (elite)
  - Protection --> on any shatter

 - Healing --> on any shatter, using heal mantra

 - Continuum Split
  - Getting good value is important to maximize boon output. With 3 clones, you have time to use all shatters, reset with Signet of Illusions, 
  then use all shatters and Time Warp.
