- More Info
 - This is a classic power reaper build. Unlike chill version, most of the damage comes from shroud skills. This is a fast paced build, designed to be played similar to Raid version. Do not camp any weapon set. 
 - Do not use axe 1 and axe 2 only situationally. Spam boonstrip skills. Use both wells together. Elite skill on CD, paired with "Nothing can save you".
 - In a regular fight, start in Axe/Sword set, once at 500-600 range (just before melee), use both wells unless tag is making that call, then shroud. If you are in GS, use GS4 before going into Shroud.
