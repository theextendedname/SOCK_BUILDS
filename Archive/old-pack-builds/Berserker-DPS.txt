  - Alternate: if you need more stability, swap Sundering Leap to Balanced Stance. Full marauder gear provides more survivability but less damage, pick per your preference.
  - Always stay on tag, wait for melee range to enter berserk. If berserk is not available, save skills until it is. Anticipate when downs will happen, use banner earlier than you think.
