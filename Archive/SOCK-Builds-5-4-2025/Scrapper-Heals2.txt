__Purpose:__
- Heal, cleanse, superspeed

__Quickguide:__
- Always be rotating through kits, weapon, and gyros. Do not camp medkit.
- Rotation timing is based around Elixir Gun 3 Fumigate's 16sec CD (see Graven's Rotation guide)
- SB arrows have a "Chain Reaction" mechanic, which adds value when another arrow is placed in the previous arrow's AOE. Order matters.
- Superspeed should be maintained as much as possible.
- Try to provide heal/barrier and cleansing together. (see Bryan's fight commentary video)

__Combos:__
- Shortbow 4-2-5 for empowering
- Shortbow 3-2-4-5 in combat
- Medkit 5-3-4-2
- Elixir Gun 3 (full channel) 5-4 (weapon swap immediately)

__Superspeed:__
- All gyros
- F1 Bandage Self

__Stab:__
- Defense Field toolbelt skill

__Stunbreak:__
- Healing Mist toolbelt skill

__Tips:__
- Switch Shortbows once Sigil of Life is fully stacked
- Drop Function Gyro and Defense Field on stragglers
- Harass siege humpers with Mortar Kit
- Elixir Gun 3 *does not* cleanse you
- Place a personal target on a party member to help targeting EG3 and SB skills
