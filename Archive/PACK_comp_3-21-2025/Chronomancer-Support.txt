 - I recommend watching this video for a fairly relevant breakdown of the build and what to focus on. This is a high apm build as you need to get good value out of your shatters and resetting shatters/utility skills with continuum split, so please be aware of that before diving in. 
 - Primary goal: Provide boons and healing to party.
 - Secondary goal: Revive downs with Illusion of Life.

Boons
Stability --> Distortion (F4) and Mantra of Concentration
Quickness --> on any shatter
Crowd Control --> Gravity Well, Focus 4

Healing --> on any shatter, using heal mantra

Contunuum Split
Getting good value is important to maximize boon output. With 3 clones, you have time to use all shatters, reset with Signet of Illusions, then use all shatters and Gravity Well.
