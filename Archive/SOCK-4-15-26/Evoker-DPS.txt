Here’s how I run the spear build and get the most out of Volcano. It’s focused on stacking as many modifiers as possible and keeping the flow clean between attunements. 

This build is meant to be on stack with stab and healing. The rotation when fighting will be different as you'll need to be moving. This just shows how I would like to do the rotations in a near perfect world.

Volcano Damage Combo Overview
──────────────────────────────
The goal of this combo is to stack as many damage modifiers as possible on Volcano for maximum output.
Only use Etching: Volcano when you can safely stay in the area for the full three casts it takes to charge it completely.
You can use Blazing Barrage before or after the combo to add extra damage.
Always trigger Seethe after using all other spear skills so its effect enhances Volcano.
The center of Meteor hits 25 percent harder, so aim carefully and try to land it on five targets within the center radius each time.

General Rotation Tips
──────────────────────
• Cast Zap and Lightning Blitz as soon as they’re off cooldown.
• Both Zap and Lightning Blitz can charge your Etching skills, so use them in place of weaker skills whenever they’re available.
• Try to start fights with your Familiar fully charged so you can open with Zap followed by Lightning Blitz.
• Use Rejuvenate immediately after casting Zap while in Earth or Water Attunement.
