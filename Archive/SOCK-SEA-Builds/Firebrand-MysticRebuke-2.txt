<:guardian_firebrand:1162443861944193054> Firebrand Boon DPS
 - [The Build](https://gw2skills.net/editor/?PWwEQLtrMCGH7ihxMxR6a/pB-DWJYnR9/h0oEoCRQ6KBP+aIEhZP8aGxlGA-w)
- More info
  - Builds focus is Stabs, Resistance, Might, Dps 
  - **Stab (Top Priority)**
     - *Skills*
       - Stand Your Ground (SYG)
       - Entering Tome of Courage (F3 book)
       - Epilogue: Unbroken Lines (skill 5 in F3 book)
       - Mantra of Liberation (elite)
       - Hallowed Ground
     - Stab skills should be followed up with cover boons from heal skill/advance
     - Stability rotation starts with SYG, then Courage once that stack has fallen off, then alternating the other options until SYG is available
     - Prioritize using Hallowed ground as the stab option when there is lag or when the commander seems like they are going to stay in one place for long
  - **Resistance (2nd priority)**
    - *Skills*
      - Spear 5
      - Stalwart Stand (F3 skill 4)
    - *Stoic Demeanor (FB trait)*
      - Stoic Demeanor requires that we CC an enemy and has a 1 second internal cooldown
         - Skills that CC
            - Spear 5
           - Shield 5
   - **Might (3rd priority)**
      -  *Might Rotation*
         - F1 (Tome 1) -> Ashes of Just (F1 Skill 5) -> Scorched Aftermath (F1 Skill 4) -> Igniting Burst (F1 Skill 2) -> Spear 3
         - Use this might rotation when the commander calls for might stacks and during the fight when F1 is available
  -  **Dps (4th priority)**
     -  Mystic Rebuke Trait would procc when aegis that we provide is broken by the enemy that allows for passive dmg while casting boons
     - *Aegis skills*
         -  Advance
         -  Restoring Reprieve (Heal Skill)
         -  Mace 3 (when hit)
         -  Shield 4
         -  Epilogue: Unbroken Lines (skill 5 in F3 book)
     - Note: space out using aegis skills for maximum dps
     -  *Spear DPS Rotation* 
        - use 5 -> 4 -> 3 if 5 is available, else 4 -> 3 
        - to take advantage of sigil of vision execute your Spear DPS rotation immediately after weapon swap. 
        
        
        Some more notes:
  - The best time for your *Spear DPS rotation* is right after finishing the **Might Rotation**. 
  - Additional information on tomes
      - **Justice (F1)**
        - skill 3 is a pull, activating Stoic Demeanor
        - skill 5 grants extra damage and might
     - **Resolve (F2)**
        - skill 2 removes conditions
        - skill 5 converts conditions to boons and increases healing done to our party
     - **Courage (F3)**
        - skill 3 is a projectile reflect dome
        - skill 4 gives pulsing resistance
        - skill 5 gives protection and stability
