  - Your main role on this build is Damage and Boon strip
  - Use Battle Standard (Elite) when called for by the commander to stomp downs.
  - Use Battle Standard (Elite) when you see 3 or more downs and stomp them.
  - Use Battle Standard (Elite) when you see 3 or more friendly downs and rez them.
  - If your tag calls for "Banner the downs" that means you.  
  - You have boon strips on your Full Counter(F2), Break Enchantments(Util Skill), and when you interrupt a foe with your hammer(Absorption Sigil).
  - Full Counter(F2) is AWSOME. It boon strips, dazes, interrupts, recharges endurance, recharges adrenaline, blocks all incoming damage, gives you stability , and is unblockable.
  - Savage Leap sword(2) is a great movement ability. Use it to keep up with tag or catch a speedy yack. 
  - Focus on using hammer burst Earthshaker(F1),  recharging your adrenaline Full Counter(F2), removing boons with Break Enchantments(Util Skill), and cleave the downs with Axe(4,5)
 
- *Zerg Combo* 
  - Hammer 3 -> Signet of Might(Util Skil) -> F1 -> F2 -> F1 -> break enchantments(Util Skill) -> F1 -> Hammer 2 -> Hammer 3 -> Swap -> Axe 4 -> Axe 5
