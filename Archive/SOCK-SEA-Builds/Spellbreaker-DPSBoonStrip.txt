  - Hammer 3 > F1 > F2 > F1 > break enchantments(Util Skill) > F1 > Hammer 2 > Hammer 3 > Swap > Axe 4 > Axe 5

- *Da Bubble casting 101*
  - Winds of Disenchantment (Elite) is a 1.5 second cast. This gives ample opportunity to get stunned, interrupted and murdered.
  - You should attempt to cast the bubble at the point of contact with the enemy. 
  - Combo the Banner of Tactics > Defiant Stance > Winds of Disenchantment (Elite)> then do your Basic Zerg Combo

- More Info
**Your Role:** Pull, Boon Strip, and Damage Dealer
**Key Abilities and Their Uses:**

* **Winds of Disenchantment (Elite - AKA: "Da Bubble"):**
  * Use when the commander calls for "Wells" or "Big Damage."
  * Strips enemy boons and blocks ranged attacks during pushes.
  * Cast at the point of enemy contact.
* **Full Counter (F2):**
  * A versatile ability:
    * Boon strip
    * Daze
    * Interrupt
    * Endurance and adrenaline recharge
    * Blocks all damage
    * Grants stability
    * Unblockable
* **Staff Snap Pull (Staff 4):**
  * Use to pull enemies into your zerg.
* **Boon Strips:**
  * Full Counter (F2)
  * Break Enchantments (Utility Skill)
  * Staff (4)
  * Hammer interrupt with Absorption Sigil.
* **Movement Skills:**
  * Staff Valiant Leap (Staff 2) and Line Breaker (Staff 3) for quick movement and damage/survivability buffs.

**General Tips:**

* Focus on executing combos correctly.
* Stay on the tag (don't stray from the group).
* Quick weapon swaps are key to this build.

**Combos:**

* **Self Buff Combo:**
  * Staff (2) > Staff (3)
  * Cast quickly at your feet.
* **Staff Pull Combo:**
  * Self Buff Combo > Staff (4) > Weapon Swap (Hammer) > Hammer (3) > Hammer (2) > Break Enchantments
  * Weapon swap should be very fast to apply Hydromancy Sigil after Staff (4).
* **Zerg Combo (Melee combat):**
  * Self Buff Combo > Staff (4) > Weapon Swap (Hammer) > Hammer (3) > Hammer (F1) > Full Counter (F2) > Hammer (F1) > Break Enchantments > Hammer(3) > Weapon swap (Staff) > Staff(F1) > Staff Pull Combo.
* **Da Bubble Combo (Engage):**
  * Banner of Tactics > Defiant Stance > Winds of Disenchantment > Hammer (3) > Hammer (F1) > Full Counter (F2) > Hammer (F1) > Break Enchantments > Hammer (3) > Weapon Swap (Staff) > Staff (F1) > Staff Pull Combo
  * Start the Zerg Combo at Hammer (3) after casting Winds of Disenchantment.
  * Winds of Disenchantment has a 1.5 second cast time, so be aware of interrupts.
