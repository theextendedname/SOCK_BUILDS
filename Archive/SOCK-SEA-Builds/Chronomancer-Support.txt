 <:mesmer_chronomancer:1162443888515100712>Chronomancer Support

- [The Build](<http://en.gw2skills.net/editor/?PiwAQx3lZwsYZMFWJesTqtUA-DWJYjRNfZkZFUdKo7vgQEm9wbx/fNA-w>)

- More info
 - Primary goal: Provide boons and healing to party.
 - Secondary goal: Revive downs with Illusion of Life.

 - Boons
  - Stability --> Distortion (F4) and Mantra of Concentration
  - Alacrity --> on any shatter
  - Quickness --> Time Warp (elite)
  - Protection --> on any shatter

 - Healing --> on any shatter, using heal mantra

 - Continuum Split
  - Getting good value is important to maximize boon output. With 3 clones, you have time to use all shatters, reset with Signet of Illusions, 
  then use all shatters and Time Warp.

  - Should be similar our support chrono role to make it more useful after you do the portal bomb.

- Portal Bomb 101 
  - Place portal entrance
  - Start casting Elite
  - While casting Elite hit F5
  - Once you have used up F5 use mimic
  - Blink towards enemy
  - Watch for stealth ending and recast elite
  - Use blink again
  - Get in position and call out for portal drop 
  - Note the direction you are facing when you drop the exit portal is the direction anyone that uses the portal will face. (try not to make us face a wall)
