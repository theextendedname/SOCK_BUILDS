- More Info:
  - Range spike (use this while pushing towards the enemy zerg, there is no animation lock so its safe to cast while running)
     - Storm spirit -> pet f2 skill -> pet f1 skill
  - Opener rotation:
     - Cast range spike -> One wolf pack(elite skill) -> Hammer 5 leaps into the sides of the zerg -> weapon swap -> mace 2 -> mace 5 -> mace 4 -> mace 3/mace 2 -> (if Nature Force activates) mace 2 and mace 5 again else wait to use mace 2/4 to activate Nature force and then use mace 2 and 5.
   - Core dps mechanics: 
      - Mace:
          - Your goal in mace/mace is to achieve 5 stacks of Nature Strength to reset your mace skills and get the Force of Nature buff to deal more dps. Prioritize Mace 5 and Mace 2.
          - Mace 3 is a leap so use at your own risk.
     - Hammer:
          - Use Hammer 4 -> Hammer 2 -> Hammer 3 for your burst. Hammer 3 has a follow up strike after the initial cast if you hit the enemy so be careful not to cancel it.
     - Pet:
          -  Cast f3 whenever you are in melee range to gain quickness and dps.
          - f2 and f1 skills should be cast together off cooldown on the enemy group.
     - Stances:
        - One Wolf Pack:
             - Gives an additional strike per 0.5s, always try to cast it during a spike.
       - Dolyak Stance:
             - Breaks stun for yourself and provides 33% dmg reduction, stabs and prevent movement condition application on allies. Use for sustaining/spiking.
       - Bear Stance:
              - Heal skill + pulsing condi cleanse on your allies.
