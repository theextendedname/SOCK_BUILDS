Role:
- provide stability through Inspiring Reinforcements (Roads)
- provide damage reduction through Rite of the Great Dwarf (Rite)
- boon strip
- incidental healing + cleansing

Fights (start at 50 energy in Dwarf stance):
- dodge regularly to keep up 10% outgoing healing buff (lasts 6s)
- use Rite as group approaches enemies
- use Energy Meld to gain energy for Roads
- legend swap to Mallyx, strip boons
- support party until you can legend swap back to Dwarf

Energy Management:
- try to be below 10 energy before you legend swap for extra 25 energy from Charged Mists (use upkeep skills if necessary)
- you can legend swap during a cast without interruption (try it with Rite)
- Energy Meld gives 25 energy, use on cooldown and have a plan to use the bonus energy

General Tips:
- prioritize utility skills over weaponskills if energy usage is tight
- always legend swap on cooldown, try to be below 10 energy
- Banish Enchantment is a beam to your target, but target does not need to be in range
- Pain Absorption pulls conditions from your party to you, be careful under siege/oil
- dodge more than you think. however much you just imagined, double that number

Please reach out if you have any questions or want training. This build is easy to play at an okay level but requires a lot of effort to optimize.
