	**1. Access the Key Bindings Menu:**

*   **In-game:** Press the `Esc` key to open the main menu.
*   **Navigate:** Go to `Options > Control Options`.

**2. Find "Set Personal Target" and "Take Personal Target":**

*   **Locate:** Scroll down the list of key bindings until you find both "Set Personal Target" and "Take Personal Target."
*   **Default:**
    *   `Set Personal Target`: Unassigned
    *   `Take Personal Target`: Unassigned

**3. Assign Keys:**

*   **Set Personal Target:**
    *   **Click:** Click in the empty box next to "Set Personal Target."
    *   **Select:** Press the key you want to assign (e.g., `Shift+F`, `F12`, `Mouse Button 4`).
*   **Take Personal Target:**
    *   **Click:** Click in the empty box next to "Take Personal Target."
    *   **Select:** Press the key you want to assign (e.g., `Shift+G`, `F5`, `Mouse Button 5`).

**4. Test Your Keybinds:**

*   **Set Target:** Select an enemy or target in-game.
*   **Press:** Press the "Set Personal Target" key.
*   **Observe:** A personal target marker should appear above your target.
*   **Take Target:** Press the "Take Personal Target" key.
*   **Observe:** Your target should immediately switch to the previously marked enemy.

**5. Tips:**

*   **Choose Keys:** Select keys that are easily accessible and don't conflict with other important keybinds.
*   **Experiment:** Try different key combinations to find what works best for your playstyle.
