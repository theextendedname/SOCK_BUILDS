- More Info
**Your Role:** Boon Support, Pull, Boon Strip, and Heal

**Key Abilities and Their Uses:**

* **Winds of Disenchantment (Elite - AKA: "Da Bubble"):**
  * Use when the commander calls for "Wells" or "Big Damage."
  * Strips enemy boons and blocks ranged attacks during pushes.
* **Full Counter (F2):**
  * A powerful ability with multiple uses:
    * Boon strip
    * Daze
    * Interrupt
    * Endurance and adrenaline recharge
    * Blocks all damage
    * Grants stability
    * Unblockable
* **Staff Snap Pull (Staff 4):**
    * Use to pull enemies into your zerg.
* **Movement Skills:**
    * Staff Valiant Leap (Staff 2), Line Breaker (Staff 3), and Sword Savage Leap (Sword 2) for quick movement.
* **To the Limit (Heal Skill):**
  * Grants endurance, heals, and cleanses.
  * Use as part of combos and after dodges to avoid enemy pushes. See "Team Dodge Combo."
* **Shake it Off (Utility Skill):**
  * AOE break free and cleanse.
  * Essential for breaking teammates free during pushes. See "Team Dodge Combo."
* **Boon Strips:**
  * Full Counter(F2)
  * Break Enchantments(Utility Skill)
  * Staff(4)

**General Tips:**

* Focus on executing combos correctly.
* Stay on the tag (don't stray from the group).
* Always cast Warhorn(4) before Warhorn(5).
* Warhorn skills have a 20 second cooldown, keep them on cooldown.


**Combos:**

* **Team Buff Combo (Might Stacking):**
    * Warhorn (4) > Warhorn (5) > Staff (2)
    * Cast every 20 seconds or when called for.
    * This is a very important combo, keep it on cooldown.
	
* **Staff Zerg Combo (Melee combat):**
    * Team Buff Combo > Staff (3) > Staff (4) > Staff (F1) > Full Counter (F2) > Staff (F1) > Break Enchantments(Utility) > To the Limit(Heal) > Team Buff Combo
    * Remember to weapon swap for warhorn skills on cooldown. Even if you have to cut the full combo short.
	* Don't skip Staff(3) as it gives 8 Seconds of Protection and needs to be cast just before engagement
	
* **Team Dodge Combo (Survival):**
    * Team Buff Combo > Dodge > Shake it Off > Dodge > To the Limit
    * Use dodge evade frames to cast Shake it Off and prevent being CC'd.
    * Use To the Limit to recover dodges.
	
* **Da Bubble Combo (Big Push):**
    * Team Buff Combo > Banner of Tactics(Utility) > Winds of Disenchantment(Elite) > Staff Zerg Combo
    * Winds of Disenchantment has a 1.5-second cast time, so be aware of interrupts.
