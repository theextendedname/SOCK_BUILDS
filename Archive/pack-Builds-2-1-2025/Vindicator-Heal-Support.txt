TLDR:
Priorities, high to low:
- Dwarf: Rite, Road
- Centaur: Energy Expulsion, Natural Harmony, Purifying Essence
- Weapons: Shield 4
- Dodge, dodge, dodge
- Energy Meld, usually in Dwarf

Role:
- Healing, damage mitigation, cleansing, stability
- Provide damage reduction through Rite of the Great Dwarf (Rite)
- Provide stability through Inspiring Reinforcements (Roads)
- Provide boons, healing, and cleansing in Centaur

Fights (start at 50 energy in Dwarf stance):
- Dodge regularly to keep up 20% outgoing healing buff and filler heal/cleanse/prot
- Use Rite as group approaches enemies
- Swap to Centaur
- Use Energy Expulsion, the centaur elite, re-deploy Tablet again immediately
- Cleanse if needed with Purifying Essence, otherwise heal with Natural Harmony
- Swap to Dwarf below 10% energy ->Rite, Road, dodge

Energy Management:
- Try to be below 10 energy before you legend swap for extra 25 energy from Charged Mists (use upkeep skills if necessary)
- You can legend swap during Rite without interruption but NOT Energy Expulsion (centaur elite)
- Energy Meld gives 25 energy, use on cooldown and have a plan to use the bonus energy

General Tips:
- Learn to love tablet management. Its utils are small, 240 radius.
- In Centaur, the first util used after deploying the tablet is "Empowered," giving them more value.
- Prioritize utility skills over weapon skills if energy usage is tight
- Always legend swap on cooldown, try to be below 10 energy
