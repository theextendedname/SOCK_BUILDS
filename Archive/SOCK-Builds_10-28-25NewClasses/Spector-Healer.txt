# HEALER SPECTER GUIDE

The Specter is a simple and strong healer that relies mostly on **Dark Aura** and **Relic of Karakosa** by blasting Wells to heal and condi cleanse allies. It also provides an insane amount of barrier.

---

## KEY CONCEPTS

* **Siphon (F1):** Siphon an ally (ideally a DPS or commander) before a fight to make them your **Shrouded Ally**. They are tethered to you in shroud and benefit from skills up to 1200 range.
* **Barrier:** Covers up to 25% of max HP in WvW. This allows the squad to absorb damage before their actual HP is affected.
* **Rot Wallow Venom:** Giving barrier grants this venom to allies. Their hits will then inflict Torment based on *your* stats.
* **Dark Combo Fields:** Wells produce these. Blasting Wells activates **Relic of Karakosa** and grants **Dark Aura**, utilizing Shadow Arts traits.
* **Boons:** Provides Protection, Stability, Vigor, Resistance, Might, and Fury.
* **Stun Break:** Blinding Powder is your primary stun break. Use it wisely.

---

## KEYBINDING RECOMMENDATIONS

* **Set/Take Personal Target:** Use this to quickly lock onto your Shrouded Ally for Siphon (F1) or Scepter skills.
* **Toggle Ally Targeting Mode:** Enables clicking on allies only.
* **Nearest Ally:** Quickly target the closest friendly player.

---

## COMBAT STRATEGY

### Pre-Engage

When stacked, use **Shadow Sap (Scepter 2)** to blast for Might. Target your Shrouded Ally and use **Endless Night (Scepter 3)** to apply barrier before the clash.

### Engage

Cycle cooldowns and adapt. Start with this rotation:

1. Well of Bounty
2. Shadow Sap x2
3. Siphon (F1)
4. Shadow Sap x2
5. Well of Silence
6. Shadow Sap
7. Enter Shadow Shroud (F2)
8. Skills 2 > 3 > 4 > 5 (or 5 > 2 > 3 > 4)
9. Exit Shadow Shroud (F2)
10. Well of Gloom
11. Shadow Sap x2
12. Siphon (F1)
13. Shadow Sap x2

### Well Utility

* **Well of Gloom:** Healing.
* **Well of Bounty:** Stability and various boons.
* **Well of Silence:** Condi cleanse.
* **Shadowfall:** CC/Pull. Use when the commander calls for a "bomb."

### Shadow Refuge

Provides burst healing and condi cleanse. Drop the AoE on allies and blast it using **Shadow Sap (Scepter 2)**.

---

### ADDITIONAL NOTES

* **Initiative Management:** Spend Initiative before entering Shroud; Shroud skills generate Initiative, and you want to avoid capping.
* **Survival:** Shroud acts as a second health bar.
* **Weapon Swap:** Swapping in combat grants 3 Initiative.
* **Flex Slot:** Shadow Refuge can replace Blinding Powder for more healing/cleanse if a stun break isn't required.
* **Positioning:** Spread out Dark Combo Fields to maximize Dark Aura blasts.
