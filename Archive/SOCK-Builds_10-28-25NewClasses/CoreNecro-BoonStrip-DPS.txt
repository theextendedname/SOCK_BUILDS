Example Burst Axe/Focus Rotation
 - Start on Axe/Focus
 - `Well of Corruption`(util)
 - `Well of Suffering`(util)
 - `Enter Death Shroud`(F1) 
 - `Dark Path`(Shroud 2) + `Tainted Shackles`(Shroud 5)
 - `Life Transfer`(Shroud 4)
 - `Dark Path`(Shroud 2) - cast second time before leaving shroud
 - `Exit Death Shroud`(F1)
 - `Unholy Feast`(Axe 3)
 - `Spinal Shivers`(Focus 5)
 - `Ghastly Claws`(Axe 2)
 - Swap to Scepter/Sword and repeat (Wells/Shroud may be on cooldown, use weapon skills as filler)

Example Burst Scepter/Sword Rotation
 - Start on Scepter/Sword

*Build Options*
 - Weapon Options Axe/Focus, Scepter/Sword.
 - Use `Signet of Vampirism` as a second, better heal; do not save it only for recovery.
 - Target enemy players at the back of the group when using `Life Blast` to maximize projectile hits.
 - Use `Doom` (Shroud 3) only on single targets without Stability for CC.
 - This build has high passive defense but low mobility and low active defense; use `Dark Pursuit` (Shroud 2) for movement.
