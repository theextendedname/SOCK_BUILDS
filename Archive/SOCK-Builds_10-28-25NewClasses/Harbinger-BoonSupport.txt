**Your Role:** Boons, Strips, and Boon corruption

- *Harbinger Boon Support* is a utility support that sets up its group with boon corruption
   - well of corruption: use on wells call
   - on engage: pop elite if available to give all boons to your party -> spam staff skills -> harbinger shroud 4 -> 5 -> 2 -> exit harbinger shroud -> Elixer of ignorance -> signet of locust -> summon blood fiend -> consume blood fiend -> use remaining staff skills.
   - swap to gs when staff skills are on cd. Hit GS Skills 5 and 4 or when commander call for pulls   
Note: for these this build signet of locust will remove 2 boons aoe, the heal skill summons blood fiend will remove 2 boons and consuming the blood fiend will remove another 2 boons so essentially people should always be consuming the blood fiend immediately after summoning it
