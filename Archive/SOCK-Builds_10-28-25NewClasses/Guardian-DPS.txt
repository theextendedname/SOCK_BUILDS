## **Class Role: Damage & Down Creation**

---

### **Core Mechanics & Buff Management**

* **Virtues (F-Skills):** Use **F1** on cooldown. If F1 is unavailable, use **F2**. Note that F1 refreshes every time an enemy dies.
* *Buff:* Activating an F-skill provides a **10% damage increase for 6 seconds.**


* **Weapon Auto-Attacks:** Spear 1 is your primary sustained damage; keep it on auto-cast or spam it constantly.
* **Illumination:** Ensure **Spear 3** and **Spear 4** are "Illuminated" before casting to maximize impact.
* *How to Illuminate:* Land a hit with **Spear 2** or stand in your **Spear 5** field.



### **Utility & Flexibility**

* **Wall of Reflection:** This is a flex slot. If the squad requires more stability, swap it for **"Stand Your Ground!"** (SYG).
* **Wall of Reflection:** This is a flex slot. If the squad requires more DAMAGE, swap it for **"Purging Flames"**.
* **Note:** Your **F3** is now a stunbreak, reducing the absolute necessity of SYG for personal survival.

---

### **Spike Rotation / Opener**

*Use this sequence on the "Wells" call or during a melee bomb:*

1. **Sword of Justice** (Cast just before the clash)
2. **Elite Skill** (Feel My Wrath)
3. **F1** (Virtue of Justice)
4. **Sword of Justice**
5. **Spear 5**
6. **Spear 4**
7. **Spear 3**
8. **Spear 1** (Spam)

### **Advanced Optimization**

If the enemy is pinned or staying directly on top of the group, perform a weapon swap for extra burst:

* Swap to **Greatsword**
* **GS 4** -> **GS 2** (twice)
* Swap back to **Spear** and resume **Spear 1** spam or the priority rotation.
