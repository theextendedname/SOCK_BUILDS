- **Important Note**: it is almost always better to save your wells for when the zerg fully commits and the enemy zerg stops maneuvering as much. Be patient in order to get maximum value from your wells.
- **Important Note**: DPS is tied to your Shroud uptime and the less dmg you take, the longer your shroud uptime. Stay on tag to reduce dmg taken.

- Shroud Mechanics
  - **Important**:  Auto attack chain reduces CD of shroud skills.
        - 3rd hit from auto attack chain in shroud reduces cooldown from all your shroud skills. When there are people to hit near you after casting shroud 4 (soul spiral),  spam 1 in shroud until  shroud 4 goes off cooldown 
           - **Tip**: Hitting downs are a good way to recharge shroud 4.
  - Boon strip on entering shroud 
       - Try to enter shroud when you are close enough to the enemy zerg to maximize boon strips
  - Additional dmg modifiers while in shroud
        - +10% dmg
    - +15% crit dmg
    - +300 ferocity
  - Removes movement impairing skills on entering shroud

- Rotations
  - Wells
     - Cast well of corruption then well of suffering before entering shroud and doing the shroud rotation
  - Shroud Rotation
      - Enter shroud when you are close to the enemy zerg -> shroud 3 -> shroud 4 -> shroud 1 autoattack rotation once (i.e. press 1 3 times so the next time when you enter shroud, shroud 4 will be up) -> exit shroud
     - Shroud 3 reduces dmg taken and provides pulsing self stabs and will not cancel shroud 4
  - When not in shroud:
    - Aim to build life force ASAP using:
    - Nothing Can Save You! (Boon strips and makes your attacks unblockable) -> Chilled to the Bone! -> Your Soul Is Mine! 
     - Axe3
     - Sword4 -> flip over skill
     - Spear5(heavy melee strip)
     - Spear3
    - Always aim to enter shroud as many times as possible during fights (but when wells is almost up, save shroud for when you drop wells)
    - Use Spear 2 off CD
     - Make sure to weapon swap off spear so you are able to use axe 3 for the boonrip and sword 4 for the chills. 
     - Stay in axe/sword while ranging since spear is a melee heavy weapon

- Additional Notes:
  - Try to use Nothing Can Save You! before Chilled To The Bone!(priority) or Your Soul Is Mine! to maximize the unblockable attacks from Nothing Can Save You!.
        - Nothing Can Save You! gives you 10 unblockable attacks ensuring that you would land your hits from: 
      - Your Soul Is Mine! for additional life force generation
      - Chilled to the Bone! for bic CC
   - Reaper has huge area of denial potential,  casting wells or strips and shouts when being pushed into will prevent us from being steamrolled.
