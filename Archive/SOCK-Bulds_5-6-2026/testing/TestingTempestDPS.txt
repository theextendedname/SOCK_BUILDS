**Main Dmg of tempest comes from overloads.**
* start in Fire 
   * Warhorn 5 for fireworks (proc relic)
   * firestorm (utility): ONLY in fire element, other CDs are too long
   * overload(fire)
   * dagger 3(if safe)
   * dagger 2 (fire) can be used on off CD
* air attunement -> spam skills 3,2,3,4 for CC 
* Conjoure Fiery Greatsword (if available)
   * Fiery Greatsword 5,1,1,1,1,1....5,1
   * while in CFG and Fire or Air overload is ready you can use overload. overload has 180 range
* when CFG is on CD, use air overload or Fire - two warhorn skill 5 casts. 
*Earth attunement 
   * Ring of earth 2
* air attunement -> spam skills 3,2,3,4 for CC 
   * overload and wait till fire is up before going back to fire and doing the dmg rotation

**Notes:**
* Use only air and fire overloads as the rest are not worth it. 
* When using Fire - Warhorn skill 5, the AOE goes in the direction your charecter is facing not to your target.
* air attunement resets its cooldown after overload is completed when you crit.
* Fiery GS gives you additional +260 power, use skill 5 and auto attack off CD. 
* Fiery GS skill 1 is a projectile and can be reflected or negated buy spellbreaker bubble.
