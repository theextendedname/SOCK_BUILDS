# SCRAPPER HEALS GUIDE

The Heal Scrapper is a strong healer in the current meta, offering excellent burst cleansing along with powerful healing and barrier generation.

---

## KEY CONCEPTS

The Heal Scrapper’s main purpose is to provide **Cleansing**, **Healing**, **Barrier Generation**, **Superspeed** and blasting fields to trigger Relic of Karakosa.

- Always rotate through your Shortbow skills, Kits, and Gyros. You can camp Med Kit (Skill 1 - Med Blaster) briefly ONLY while waiting for other skills to get off cooldown.
- The rotation is built around Elixir Gun (Skill 3 - Fumigate), your goal is to use Fumigate as often as possible. When swapping back to Elixir Gun, Fumigate or Super Elixir (Skill 5) should be just coming off cooldown.
- You should maintain **Superspeed** whenever possible.
  - When out of combat, **Superspeed** = **Swiftness**.
  - When in combat, **Superspeed** = 100% increase to forward movement speed.

__Combos:__
- Shortbow 4 > 2 > 5  when commander calls for **Might** 
- **Bulwark Gyro** as we push in, then switch to **Elixir Gun** 3 > 5 > 4 (Quickly weapon swap after using Skill 4 to cancel backward leap) as soo as we engage.
- **Med Kit** 5 > 3 > 2 > 4 *OR* **Med Kit** 3 > 5 > 2 > 4 (If ping is a problem).
- **Shortbow** 3 > 2 > 4 > 5 (Skill 2 and Skill 5 are blast finishers).

__Chain Reaction:__
The Chain Reaction mechanic is unique to the engineer's Shortbow. These are example of what you'll get when you chain react.
- Shortbow 3 > 2 (Condi Cleanse)
- Shortbow 4 > 5 (Grant Boons)

__Superspeed:__
- All Gyros
- Bandage Self (F1)
- Shortbow (Skill 5)

__Party Stability/Block Missiles:__
- Defense Field (F4)

__Personal Breaks Stun:__
- Healing Mist (F2)

---

### ADDITIONAL NOTES

- Switch to your **Main Shortbow** once you reach 25 stacks of Life on your **Stacking Shortbow**.
- **Superior Sigil of Frenzy** is an alternative option for **Superior Sigil of Renewal**.
- Be sure to use **Supply Crate** (Elite Skill) to stun enemies and blast your water fields.
- Drop **Chemical Field** (F3) to poison downed enemies.
- Use **Purge Gyro** when additional cleanse is required.
- Provide **Superspeed** to allies who fall behind.
- Elixir Gun (Skill 3 - Fumigate) cleanses you and your party!
- Put a **Personal Target** on a party or squad member to help guide your Shortbow skill placement.
