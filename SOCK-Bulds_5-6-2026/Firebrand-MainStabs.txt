 - Builds focus is Stabs, Resistance, Might
 - **Stab (Top Priority)**
    - *Skills*
        - Stand Your Ground (SYG)
    - Entering Tome of Courage (F3 book)
        - Epilogue: Unbroken Lines (skill 5 in F3 book)
    - Mantra of Liberation (elite)
    - Hallowed Ground
    - Stab skills should be followed up with cover boons from heal skill/advance
    - Stability rotation starts with SYG, then F3 once stab is gone, then other options until SYG is available
    - Prioritize using Hallowed ground with lag or when the commander is going to stay in one place
 - **Resistance (2nd priority)**
 - *Skills*
    - Spear 5
  - Stalwart Stand (F3 skill 4)
 - *Stoic Demeanor (FB trait)*
    - Stoic Demeanor requires that we CC an enemy and has a 1 second internal cooldown
        - Skills that CC
            - Spear 5
      - Shield 5
 - **Might (3rd priority)**
    -  *Might Rotation*
        - F1 (Tome 1) -> Ashes of Just (F1 Skill 5) -> Scorched Aftermath (F1 Skill 4) -> Igniting Burst (F1 Skill 2) -> Spear 3
      - Use this when the commander calls for might stacks and during the fight when F1 is available
Some more notes:
 - you want to ideally swap to spear once F1 is ready to do your might stack after using spear 5 -> 4 -> 3 or 3 -> 4 so you keep the might high in your subgroup
 - Additional information on tomes
  - **Justice (F1)**
    - skill 3 is a pull, activating Stoic Demeanor
    - skill 5 grants extra damage and might
 - **Resolve (F2)**
    - skill 2 removes conditions
    - skill 5 converts conditions to boons and increases healing done to our party
 - **Courage (F3)**
    - skill 3 is a projectile reflect dome
    - skill 4 gives pulsing resistance
    - skill 5 gives protection and stability
 - Stab Rotations
    - SYG -> Advance! -> heals
  - F3 -> Epilogue: Unbroken Lines (Skill 5) ->  Stalwart Stand (Skill 4) -> Advance! -> heals(if available)
