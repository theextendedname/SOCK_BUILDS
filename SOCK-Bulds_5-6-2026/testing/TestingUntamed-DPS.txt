-  **Untamed Mechanics:**
    - Untamed mechanic: Revolves around switching between Unleashed states for you and your pet to boost damage and abilities.
    - Unleashed Player: Access to powerful Ambush skills (burst damage, boon strip). 
    - Unleashed Pet: Gains new abilities like Venomous Outburst, Rending Vines, and Enveloping Haze. 
    - Cycle Unleash **Pet Combo:**: Every 15-20 seconds to utilize Unleashed Power Passive, boon strips and Enveloping Haze.
    - The Unleashed Power Passive: For 4 seconds after swapping to Unleased Ranger. You gain access to Ravager's Abbandon(Spear 1) and Relentless Whirl(Hammer 1). These are high damage and powerful.

-  **Pet Choices:**
    - Brown Bear: Damage immunity, condition cleanse
    - Polar Bear:  Damage immunity, AoE chill & stun
    - Melo: Damage immunity, poison AoE heal reduction


-  **Pet Tip:** Set pet to avoid combat and manually control skills

-  **Weapon Sets:**
    - Spear: Focus on AoE pressure and burst
    - Hammer: Primarily use the Unleashed skills for higher damage and boon strip

-  **Other Key Info:**
    - Exploding Spores: Use frequently to deal high damage, strip bounds, and apply poison
    - Forest's Fortification: Use strikes to reduce its cooldown, %50 incoming damage reduction, combo with Exploding Spores for burst damage
    - Signate of the Wild: activate for a damage boost


-  **Pet Combo:**
    - Select a Target -> Unleash Pet -> Pet(F1, F2,F3) -> Unleashed Ranger

-  **Spear Combo:**
    -  Ravager's Abandon/Spear(1) + Spear(5) + Use Spear Ambush(2 Or 4)

-  **Hammer Combo:**
    - Forest's Fortification -> Relentless Whirl(Hammer 1) -> Exploding Spores(util) -> Hammer(5) -> Signate of the Wild(util) -> Hammer(4, 2, 3) -> Hammer (1,1,1)

-  **One key Combo**
    - **Pet Combo** -> Ravager's Abandon/Spear(1) -> Weapon Swap ->  Relentless Whirl(Hammer 1)

-  **Full Rotation:**
    - **Pet Combo** -> **Spear Combo** -> **Hammer Combo**
    -  ________________________________________________________    
    - Select a Target -> Unleash Pet -> Pet(F1, F2,F3) -> Unleashed Ranger ->  Ravager's Abandon/Spear(1) -> Spear(5) + Use Spear Ambush(2 Or 4), Weapon swap to Hammer -> Forest's Fortification -> Relentless Whirl(Hammer 1) -> Exploding Spores(util) -> Hammer(5) -> Signate of the Wild(util) -> Hammer(4, 2, 3) -> Hammer (1,1,1)


-  **Mid-Combat:**
    - Alternate between spear and hammer rotations, using the listed skills to maintain a consistent damage flow.
   -  Cycle Unleash **Pet Combo**: Every 15-20 seconds to utilize Unleashed Power Passive, boon strips and Enveloping Haze.
    -  Use the **One key Combo** if your other skills are on cooldown
