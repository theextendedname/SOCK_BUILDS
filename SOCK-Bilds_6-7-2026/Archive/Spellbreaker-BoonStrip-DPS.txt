 * Your main role on this build is Damage and Boon strip
 * Use your Spear to spike enemies at range
 * Strip boons when the group mele spikes the enemy
 * Use your melee weapons to finish enemies
 * Do not camp a weapons. Weapon swapping reduces your `Burst` cooldowns, gives you swiftness and boosts your damage by 10%
 * `Full Counter` (F2) is OP.  Endurance Gain, Adrenaline Restore, Grants Swiftness, Does Damage, Reduces your incoming Damage by 100%, Grants Stability, Grants evasion, Dazes enemies, Strips Boons. Please learn to us this Burst Skill it is amazing. 
* *Pro Tip* `For Full Counter to activate you must get hit and be within 300 range of the attacker. It is a MELEE burst skill!!`

 * Winds of Disenchantment (elite)

  ***Boon Strip***
 *  Land `Winds of Disenchantment` (elite) on the enemy group while your group is in range to follow up with a spike. You can do this either as your group pushes into the enemy group or as the enemy group pushes into your group. In either case it ISN'T usually a good idea to go far from your own group during the cast.
 *  You should be casting `Break Enchantments` (utility) as often as possible, ideally right before either you or your group spikes the enemy group
 * `Break Enchantments`(utility) is a great way to strip boons and proc Loss Aversion to give you enough Adrenaline for Arcing Slice or Harrier's Toss 
 * Spellbreaker as many passives and skill synergies that generate boon strip and boost damage. 
      * `Dispelling Force` (passive) when you daze an enemy with `Disrupting Throw` (Spear 3) you also strip a boon. That boon strip gives you adrenaline from the `Loss Aversion` trait. You also get 2 stacks of `Attacker's Insight` (trait) and strip up to 5 near by foes of one boon. Which gives even more `Attacker's Insight` (trait). There are more synergies at play here but you should get the idea.
