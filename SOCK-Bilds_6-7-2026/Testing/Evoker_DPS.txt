 - [Build](https://gw2skills.net/editor/?PGgEQjWGMLhdxoYn4orsNrUCHAA-DWRYiRVn3cQhFSTFRdhQFZgtnFCQA7h3i+nRAA-w)
 - [Game Play Video](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=Y11-oPiQFfg)
 - Weapon Options: Spear
 - Always start in Air Attunement for high Range and early Crowd Control
 - Swap to Fire Attunement next to line up with the squad's Well of Corruption Spike
 - Follow up instantly with Conjure Fiery Greatsword to keep dealing massive damage
 - Swap to Earth Attunement to maintain damage while waiting for Air and Fire Etchings to clear Cooldowns

*Burst Damage Skill List*
 - Fire - `Meteor`(4) (Biggest damage skill, use twice with Arcane Echo)
 - Fire - `Blazing Barrage`(2) (Short cooldown, high damage)
 - Fire - `Volcano`(5) (Massive pulsing damage in a large radius)
 - Earth - `Fissure`(4) (Main source of burst in Earth)
 - Earth - `Haboob`(5) (Massive wave damaging and blinding enemies)
 - Air - `Derecho`(5) (Massive wave damaging and dazing enemies)
 - Air - `Fulgor`(2) (High damage, unique uncleansable effect)

*Damage Modifier Skill List*
 - Fire - `Seethe`(3) (Grants a damage increase to your next Spear skill)
 - Air - `Energize`(3) (Instant cast, ensures next Spear skill is a Critical Hit)

*Utility & Crowd Control Skill List*
 - Water - `Undertow`(4) (Strong pull that inflicts Slow to catch players out of stack)
 - Air - `Twister`(4) (Strong crowd control to isolate players)
 - Earth - `Harden`(3) (Instant cast, grants Barrier and inflicts Daze)
 - `Zap`(F5) (1200 range - Use on cooldown)
 - `Rejuvenate`(Heal) (Use just after `Zap` to recharge `Zap`)
 - Earth - `Earthen Spear`(2) (Use to hit players out of Reflect Projectiles)

Example Burst Spear Rotation
 - **Start in Air Attunement**
 - `Zap` (1200 range - Use on Cooldown)
 - `Rejuvenate`(Heal) (Use 5 sec after `Zap` to recharge `Zap`) 
 - `Etching: Derecho`
 - `Energize`
 - `Twister`
 - `Fulgor`
 - `Derecho`
 - **Swap to Fire Attunement**
    * `Conjure Fiery GreatSword` **(if not on CD and during a Well call from tag)**
    * `Firestorm` **(Glyph Utility)**    
    * `Firestorm` **(From CFG) Press once and drop CFG**
 - `Blazing Barrage`
 - `Etching: Volcano`
 - `Firestorm`(Glyph Utility)
 - `Seethe` + `Arcane Echo` + `Meteor` (Always use Seethe before Meteor)
 - `Volcano`
 - `Blazing Barrage`
 - `Meteor`
 - **Swap to Earth Attunement**
 - `Etching: Haboob`
 - `Harden` (Good way to complete etching quickly)
 - `Fissure`
 - `Earthen Spear`
 - `Haboob`
 - **Swap to Water Attunement**
 - `Ice Beam` -> `Ripple`(Skip if you don't need movement) -> `Undertow`
 - **Swap to Air Attunement** and repeat (Earth won't be up yet on the second pass)

*Rotation Notes & Build Options*
 - Due to high range, follow the main rotation easily but adapt to zerg volatility.
 - After your first burst loop, Earth Attunement won't be accessible for a while. 
 - Stay longer in Air and Fire Attunements to wait for Etchings. Cast `Fulgor` earlier in Air for a second cast, and hold Fire for another `Blazing Barrage` cast to avoid lowering DPS.
 - Remember that you can complete Etchings using skills outside of your Spear like `Arcane Brilliance`, `Glyph of Storms`, and even your Auto Attack Chains!
