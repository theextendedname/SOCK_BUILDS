Example Burst Hammer Rotation
 - Start on Hammer 
 - `Thunderclap`(Hammer5)
 - `Plasmatic State`(util)
 - `FluxState`(elite)
 - `Thorns(F4)` + `Obliterate`(F2) + `Demolish`(F3)
 - `Evolve`(F5)
 - `Obliterate`(F2) + `Demolish`(F3) + `Thorns`(F4)
 - Swap to Elixir Gun 
 - `Acid Bomb`(EG4) + Weapon Swap to Flamethrower (animation canceling the Leap Back)
 - Swap to Flamethrower  -> `Flame Blast`(FT2) -> `Napalm`(FT5)  
 - Flamethrower/auto(1) -> `Flame Blast`(FT2) until Protocols are off cool down. Then Swap to Hammer and repeat(evolve wont be up yet so you only get to hit F4+F2+F3 once on the second pass)
