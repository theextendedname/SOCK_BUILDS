# **PLAYSTYLE & ROLE OVERVIEW**

> **Power Scourge** is a utility DPS that sets up its group with heavy boon corruption.

* **The Spike:** Your highest priority is using `Well of Corruption` and `Well of Suffering` together with other Necromancers in the squad on the commander's call. This creates the window for your group to secure kills.
* **Secondary Wells:** Use `Ghastly Breach` and `Nightfall` just like wells—at the same time, and in that exact order, on the commander's call. If your commander doesn't call them, use them reactively to punish the enemy group pushing into yours.
* **Extra Damage:** Use `Manifest Sand Shade` => `Desert Shroud` to layer extra damage on top of your other group spikes.
* **Crowd Control:** Save `Grasping Darkness` to pull enemies into your group's spikes, or to catch enemies trying to kite away.
* **Weapons:** Focus on using **Axe/Focus** until you actively need `Nightfall` or `Grasping Darkness` on **Greatsword**.

---

# **SHADE MANAGEMENT (F1-F5)**

* **Maintain Presence:** Having at least **one active shade** at all times is critical. Thanks to *Blood as Sand*, this reduces your incoming damage by 7%. Do *not* spam shades; dropping them one at a time is much more efficient.
* **Double Coverage:** All shade skill effects occur on **both the shade AND you**. This allows you to place shades on the enemy's path while still guaranteeing cleanses for your immediate team.
* **Cast Time:** Shade skills are instant cast. Weave them into your rotation on cooldown while fighting.

---

# **VS CLOUD (SPREAD OUT ENEMIES)**

*Recommendations above are aimed toward traditional squad-vs-squad. If fighting clouds, adjust with the following:*

* **Shade Importance:** Shades are even more vital here. You can almost upkeep 2 shades at all times. The ability to hit multiple places at once and control chokepoints from a distance is extremely strong. Fights in SMC and Keeps are excellent for Scourge.
* **The Killer Combo:** Wells by themselves are strong, but combined with shades, they generate instant downs.
* **The Combo:** Place wells where your shades are located => hit `F2`/`F3`/`F4` (instant cast) => hit `Focus 5`. Most players without invulnerability will immediately go down.


* **Kiting Defense:** Use `Trail of Anguish` frequently when moving. It helps you, supports your teammates, and strips enemy boons. Any groups trying to pick off the tail of your squad will step right into it.
