**Skill priority**
 * Well of Corruption + Well of Suffering - always together, on call
 * Ghastly Breach/Nightfall - on call, not always together
 * Unholy Feast
 * Manifest Sand Shade ⇒ Desert Shroud + any other Shade skills that aren't on cooldown
 * Spinal Shivers ⇒ Ghastly Claws - only on single target
 * Death Spiral
 * Gravedigger
 * Soul Grasp - only if low on Life Force
 
- *Power Scourge* is a utility DPS that sets up its group with boon corruption
   - Your highest priority is using your Well of Corruption and Well of Suffering together with other necros in squad on the commanders call to create an opportunity for your group to generate kills
   - Use Nightfall and Ghastly Breach like wells - at the same time as other necros on the commanders call
   - If your commander doesn't call these skills, use them to punish the enemy group pushing into yours
   - Use Manifest Sand Shade -> Desert Shroud to add extra damage on top of your other spikes
   - Save Grasping Darkness to Pull Pull enemies into your groups spikes or catch enemies when they try to kite your group
   - Focus on using Axe/Warhorn until you need Nightfall or Grasping Darkness on Greatsword


VS Cloud. Recommendations above are aimed towards traditional squad vs squad. If we are fighting clouds we need to adjust.
Shades are even more important. You can almost upkeep 2 shades at all time. The ability to hit multiple places at once and control chokepoints from distance is extremely strong. Fights in SMC and keeps are excellent for Scourge.
Wells. Wells by themselves are very strong, but combined with shades can generate downs. The combo is, place wells where your shades are located, hit F2/3/4 (instant cast) then hit Focus 5. Most players without invulnerability go down.
Use Trail of Anguish often when we are moving. It helps you, your teammates and strips boons. Any groups trying to pick the tail will step on it.
